  • Meedee
    MIDI Platformer Game
    Meedee is a procedurally generated 2D platforming game. Levels are generated from a user-provided MIDI file, which the game uses to construct terrain based on its contents. The MIDI file's composition is played in time with the player's movements as they progress through the procedurally-generated level.
    Made With: Python & PyGame

  • Thisorthat
    Trivia Maze Game
    Thisorthat is a trivia maze game developed for TCSS 308 Software Development by two classmates and I at University of Washington. The goal is to escape the labyrinthine halls of Ikea by correctly answering trivia questions. Each question gives a subject, and the possible answers are different groupings the subject may belong to.
