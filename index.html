Works
  • Lange Studios
    Recording Studio Website
    The Lange Studios website is a piece of freelance work done for the titular recording studio in Puyallup, WA. The website features interactive client audio samples, a guide to the recording process, pricing information, contact information, a list of all available equipment, a gallery of images & prior video shoots from the recording studio, and an overview of the studio's 40+ year history. The website was designed to be responsive, asthetically pleasing, easy to navigate, and well representative of the studio to potential clients.
    Made With: React, JavaScript, Gatsby, Photoshop, & Illustrator

  • Synesthesia
    Music Platformer Game
    Synesthesia is an abstract cat platformer featuring a dynamic audio system, syncing music with the player's movement in real-time. Synesthesia's visuals are a mix of traditional pixel art and PS1-inspired 3D backgrounds. Synesthesia has been played over 20,000 times across Itch.io, Newgrounds, SAGE, Indie DB, and Game Jolt. Builds for HTML5, Windows, Linux, and macOS are available for free on Itch.io.
    Made With: Godot, GDScript, Photoshop, Illustrator, & Aseprite

  • Thisorthat
    Trivia Maze Game
    Thisorthat is a trivia maze game developed for TCSS 308 Software Development by two classmates and I at University of Washington. The goal is to escape the labyrinthine halls of Ikea by correctly answering trivia questions. Each question gives a subject, and the possible answers are different groupings the subject may belong to.
